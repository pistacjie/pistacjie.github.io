About us

Pistacjie Gelato House is the best gelato house in town. Pistacjie first opened on 1 September 2022 in Bogor. All recipes used are original recipes from Pistacjie, cooked using quality and natural ingredients, processed in the factory with special techniques and controlled with strict quality assurance standards in our shop.

The menu at Pistacjie Gelato House has been verified as halal and BPOM sertificated

Pistacjie not only provides gelato, but also presents an interesting and quality side menu
